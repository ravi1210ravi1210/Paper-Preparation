Q1. Explain objectives and features of IT Act 2000.

Answer:

  • Provides legal recognition to electronic records and digital signatures.
  • Defines cybercrimes and penalties.
  • Establishes a Cyber Appellate Tribunal.
  • Promotes secure e-commerce and digital transactions.

  • Hacking: Unauthorized system access.
  • Phishing: Fake websites to steal data.
  • Cyber Stalking: Harassing individuals online.

Q3. What are Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)? Explain its types.

  • Copyright: Protects creative works (books, music).
  • Patent: Protects inventions.
  • Trademark: Protects brand names and logos.
  • Designs: Protects visual appearance of a product.
  • Trade Secret: Protects confidential business information.
